# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard & Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project delivers an end-to-end data analysis solution designed to transform raw, unorganized e-commerce transactional records into actionable business intelligence. Utilizing **T-SQL (MS SQL Server)** for data cleaning, transformation, and structural analysis, alongside **Power BI / Excel**, the project translates complex metrics into an intuitive, interactive dashboard layout. 

The analysis tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) across regional performance, category breakdowns, monthly trends, and high-value products to empower stakeholder decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights & KPIs
Based on the data processed, the e-commerce platform generated the following high-level metrics over its operational lifecycle:
* **Total Sales Revenue:** 10.67M
* **Total Profit:** 1.84M
* **Overall Profit Margin:** 17.29%
* **Total Units Sold:** 3,501 units
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 3.05K

### Strategic Observations:
* **Top Performing Region:** The **West** region leads performance with **2.84M** in total sales, closely trailed by the East and South regions. 
* **Dominant Categories:** **Electronics** stands out as the primary revenue engine, generating **5.33M** in sales, followed by Accessories (**4.25M**). Office supplies constitute a smaller segment at **1.09M**.
* **Product Superstars:** The top revenue-generating individual products are **Cameras** (1.18M) and **Monitors** (1.16M).
* **Seasonality & Trends:** Monthly tracking reveals distinct sales peaks, with **May** emerging as a highly lucrative month tracking over **1.03M** in individual monthly sales.
